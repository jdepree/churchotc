The Space

A beautiful mountain setting, 30 minutes west of Denver.

Church of the Cross offers a warm, welcoming venue for a wide variety of private events. Whether you're planning an intimate gathering or a larger celebration, our facilities can accommodate 3 to 100 guests.

We're available for:

  • Weddings & vow renewals
  • Baptisms & baby showers
  • Reunions & family gatherings
  • Graduation celebrations
  • Celebrations of life & memorial services
  • Birthday parties & retirement celebrations
  • Fundraisers & nonprofit events
