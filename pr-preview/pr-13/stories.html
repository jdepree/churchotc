Finding Our Kingdom Concept

In a collaborative workshop facilitated by Bridge Church consultant Nate, our leadership team generated 240 words that described our hopes for Church of the Cross — and whittled them down to roughly 20 words of shared consensus. From that process emerged our vision ("Everyone experiences the love of God") and our mission ("We are dedicated to renewing lives and communities by making vibrant and loving disciples of Jesus").
