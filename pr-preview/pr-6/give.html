Ways to Give

The Three T's of Giving

Generosity takes many forms. We invite you to prayerfully consider how you are called to support the work of God through Church of the Cross.

T

Time

One of the most meaningful gifts you can give is your presence. Show up on Sunday mornings. Follow us on social media. Share our content. Sign up for our newsletter and stay connected to what God is doing in and through our congregation. Every act of engagement is a gift.

YouTube Facebook Newsletter
T

Talents

God has uniquely gifted each of us for a purpose. Whether your gifts are in music, teaching, hospitality, administration, technology, building, or something else entirely — there is a place for you to serve at Church of the Cross. We believe every ministry is strengthened when members bring their God-given abilities to the table.

Volunteer Your Talents
T

Treasures

Financial generosity is an act of faith and worship. Your financial gifts support our weekly ministry, our outreach programs, our youth work, and our ongoing mission to share the love of Christ in Evergreen and beyond. We offer secure online giving through Vanco.

"Each one must give as he has decided in his heart, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver."

2 Corinthians 9:7

You may also give by check made payable to Church of the Cross, mailed to or dropped off at 28253 Meadow Drive, Evergreen, CO 80439.
