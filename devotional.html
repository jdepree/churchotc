"I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me."

Philippians 4:13

Strength Beyond Our Own

There are moments when life feels too heavy — when the challenges in front of us seem to outpace the strength within us. Paul wrote these words from prison, not from a place of comfort. His confidence wasn't in his own ability, but in the One who walks with us through every season.

Today, bring whatever feels heavy to God in prayer. Ask Him to renew your strength, and trust that His power is made perfect in our weakness (2 Corinthians 12:9).

Lord, thank You that I don't have to face today alone. Strengthen me through Your Spirit, and help me lean on You in every moment. Amen.

"Be still, and know that I am God."

Psalm 46:10

A Moment of Stillness

In our busy, fast-paced lives, stillness can feel like a luxury. But God invites us into it — not as an escape, but as an encounter. To be still is to release our grip on control and acknowledge that God is God, and we are not.

Take a few quiet moments today. Put down your phone. Step away from the noise. Simply rest in the knowledge that the God who holds the universe in His hands also holds you.

Father, quiet my heart and mind. Help me to trust You with what I cannot control and to find peace in Your presence. Amen.

"For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life."

John 3:16

The Heart of the Gospel

These may be the most familiar words in all of Scripture — and yet they never grow old. At the center of everything we believe is this: God loves. God gives. God saves. Not because we earned it, but because love is who He is.

Let this truth anchor you today. No matter what you are going through, you are known and loved by the Creator of all things. That love sent Jesus — and it holds you now.

Thank You, God, for Your incredible love. May I never take for granted the gift of Your Son, and may that love overflow into everything I do today. Amen.
