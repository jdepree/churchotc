Every Sunday

Join Us for Worship

We gather every Sunday at 9:30 AM for a service that blends contemporary praise music with the richness of traditional Lutheran liturgy. Our services are welcoming to people of all backgrounds — from lifelong Christians to curious seekers.

Can't make it in person? Join us via live stream on YouTube. Past services are also available on our YouTube channel.

What to Expect

A Typical Sunday Service

Here's what a Sunday morning at Church of the Cross looks like. You're welcome to come as you are — dress is casual to business casual.

  • Praise songs and contemporary worship music
  • Children's lesson and blessing (children are then invited to Children's Church)
  • Scripture reading
  • Message (sermon)
  • Confession of sins and words of absolution
  • Holy Communion (all baptized believers welcome)
  • Prayer for the congregation and community
  • Sending hymn and benediction
Summer

Outdoor Worship Among the Pines

During the summer months, we take worship outside — gathering among Evergreen's beautiful ponderosa pines for open-air services. It's one of the most beloved traditions at Church of the Cross.

And yes — pets are welcome at our outdoor summer services. Bring the whole family.

Check our calendar for this season's outdoor worship schedule.
