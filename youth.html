Youth Ministry

We provide a safe space for mountain youth to grow in faith, friendship and purpose.

Come as you are.

Our Youth Ministry relies on partnership with the local Fellowship of Christian Athletes, Young Life, and the local Boy Scouts chapter, which meets in our building on Tuesday evenings.

Join us in prayer for the Church of the Cross to be an intergenerational church that is a light to students of all ages in the Evergreen area.

Early Childhood

Evergreen Christian Academy

Church of the Cross is proud to be home to Evergreen Christian Academy, a licensed early childhood program serving families with children from 6 weeks through Kindergarten.

ECA offers a nurturing, faith-centered environment where children learn and grow through play, relationships, and Christian values. The Academy is open year-round — including summers — making it a reliable and loving childcare option for Evergreen families.

  • Ages: 6 weeks through Kindergarten
  • Hours: 6:30 AM – 6:00 PM
  • Schedule: Year-round, including summers
  • Enrollment: Open enrollment
