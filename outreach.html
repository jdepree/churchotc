How We Serve

Putting Faith Into Practice

We believe the church is most alive when it's giving itself away. Here's how we serve our neighbors — near and far.

✈️

Mission

We organize group mission trips to Honduras, Belize, Uganda, and locally — partnering with communities to address real needs in the name of Christ. These trips change lives on both sides.

🙌

Service

Members volunteer regularly at local Christian organizations and community projects throughout the Evergreen area. Whether it's a food pantry, a shelter, or a community cleanup, we show up.

☀️

Son-shine

Small acts of love make a big difference. Our Son-shine ministry sends birthday and anniversary cards, bakes farmer's market pies, collects toys for children globally, and carols at local senior facilities.
