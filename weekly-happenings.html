Mission Trip

Belize Mission Trip 2026

Come join Pastor Tyler and Kathy Hassell on a mission trip to Belize this summer!

The dates will be June 6–13 or July 4–11, depending on which work best for those who feel a call to go.

The mission organization is World Gospel Outreach (WGO); information can be found at www.wgoreach.org.

We'll be:

  • Giving away medicines and reading glasses
  • Teaching children about Jesus
  • Praying with people
  • Visiting homes to provide the blessing of food, water filters, and prayer
  • Completing light projects for the churches and villages we serve

No medical skills needed — just a passion for sharing the love of Jesus with the "least of these"!

Please contact Kathy Hassell to hear more about it!

You can message/call Kathy at 303-229-7346 or email her at kathy.hassell@wgomission.org.
